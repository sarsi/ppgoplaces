Important - Add Location

INDOOR option

  • Turn OFF GPS when indoor to preserve battery life, GPS can only operate outdoor with a clear line of sight to the orbiting satellites.
  • Turn ON WiFi when there are wireless network or mobile hotspots available, it will enhance location search even user is not sign on to any wireless network.

    • OUTDOOR option

  • Turn ON GPS when outdoor in open field, beach or country side, where there is clear view to the sky. User may wants to turn OFF WiFi to preserve battery life when there is no mobile hotspots coverage in the area,

  • Turn ON WiFi when in downtown or city where lot of tall buildings and trees, it may be difficult to get a location fix by GPS.

    • Stop Location Updates button

  • Stop Location Updates button will display once the first location is found.
  • Once a good location fix and further location/address updates is no longer required, stop location updates to preserve battery life.

    • Note: The information provided here is base upon experiences using AndroidTM location services, and is by no means representative of the functionality and usability of location services. The experience shared here is intends to help improve user experience of using this application.
